Find the source code at: github.com/felixrabe/MathTetris.

To move or turn the falling piece, click and hold the mouse on the game board, then move the mouse in the direction you want to use: left or right or down, or move up to turn the piece clockwise.

The game is called MathTetris, because I wanted the user to solve a math puzzle when a line of blocks is full, but I have not implemented that yet.

Have fun! - Felix Rabe
